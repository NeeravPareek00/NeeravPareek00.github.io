Jaigardh Fort

Jaigarh Fort is situated on thepromontory called the Cheel ka Teela(Hill of Eagles) of the Aravallirange. it overlooks the AmerFort and the MaotaLake, near Amer inJaipur, Rajasthan,India.The fort wasbuilt by Jai Singh IIin 1726 to protect theAmer Fort.

Amer fort

Amer Fort is a hill fortlocated on a high hill in the Amerregion of Jaipur , the capital ofthe Indian state of Rajasthan . Itis the main tourist attraction ofJaipur city. Kachwaha Rajput ManSingh I built this fort. This fort and palace is also known for its artistic elements ofpurely Hindu architectural style.Mawtha Sarovar , built just belowthe fort hillseems to be lookingat.

Jal mahal

Jalmahal is a famous historical palace situated in the middle of Mansagar Lake in Jaipur , the capital of Rajasthan . Situated in the womb of the Aravalli hills, this palace is also called 'Eye Ball'due to being in the middle of the lake . It was also known as 'Romantic Palace'. Built by Jai Singh, this palace is a two-storeyed and square-shaped building with arches, bastions, chhatris and stepped genes like the medieval palaces. Jalmahal is now also developing as a bird sanctuary. More than 1 lakh trees have been planted in the nursery here where the tallest trees of Rajasthan arefound.
